Decisions I made — tell me what to change

I didn't stop to ask, so these are my calls. Each is a one-line tweak if you disagree.

Decision 1 · Schedule

Split view, no auto-select

iPad shows the list + detail side by side. When nothing's picked yet, the detail pane shows a "Select a match" placeholder.

My call: placeholder, don't auto-open a match (avoids accidentally spoiling a score on launch).
Alternative: auto-select the next upcoming match so the pane is never empty.
Decision 2 · Teams

6 columns on iPad, grid not split

Teams stays a visual flag grid (just wider) rather than becoming a list+detail split. Width-driven, default lands ~6 portrait / ~8 landscape.

My call: grid feels right for flags; keep push-to-detail.
Alternative: make Teams a split view too (like Schedule). Use the slider above to pick your column count.
Decision 3 · Standings

Two groups per row

Expanded tables go 2-up on iPad; condensed mini-tables 3–4 across. iPhone untouched.

My call: 2-up for full tables — readable without feeling cramped.
Alternative: 3-up full tables for even more density.
Decision 4 · Readable width

~680–820 pt, centered

Menus/settings cap at 680, detail screens a bit wider (760–820). Centered in the pane.

My call: centered column — standard iPad reading width.
Alternative: different cap, or left-align instead of center.
Decision 5 · Tab bar

Left the bottom tab bar as-is

iPadOS can turn the tab bar into a sidebar, but that's iOS 18+ and would collide with the trivia tab work. Skipped for now.

My call: don't touch the tab bar / ContentView (trivia session owns it).
Alternative: revisit a sidebar-style tab bar later, once trivia merges.
Decision 6 · Formations

Taller pitch on iPad

The formation pitch grows from 440→560pt tall on iPad so it uses the room. Player dots unchanged.

My call: a modest height bump, not a full redesign.
Alternative: make it fully proportional / even larger.
