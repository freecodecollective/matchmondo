Rec Recommended approach
Combine A + B: The "Predict mode" toggle (Option A) turns the existing bracket into a pick-your-winner interface. Picks cascade forward. Once matches finish, the result cards (Option B) appear inline showing ✓/✗ and points. The scoring summary sits below the bracket.

Implementation notes:
• Reuse existing PredictionStore + Firestore backend — just add bracket picks alongside match-level picks.
• Bracket picks are a separate concept from exact-score picks in the Fun→Predictions tab (those give H/D/A + score; these just pick the winner).
• Lock picks per match at kickoff time (same logic as current predictions).
• Purple styling keeps picks visually separate from green actual results.
