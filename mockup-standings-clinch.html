What "we know" actually means (as of 21 Jun)

Through — guaranteed a top-2 finish: Mexico (A), USA (D), Germany (E). Each has 6 pts with one game left, and their groups can't produce two teams that overtake them — so a top-2 place is locked regardless of results.

Out — cannot advance: Haiti (C), Türkiye (D), Tunisia (F). All sit on 0 pts and can no longer reach the top 2; the only mathematical lifeline (sneaking in as a best-3rd team) would need a 5–7 goal blowout in their last match and other results to fall perfectly — so we treat them as eliminated.

Everyone else stays neutral:

  • Groups A–F — second place and the best-3rd race are still genuinely open, so no team beyond the six above is colored.
  • Groups G–L — only matchday 1 has been played (2 of 6 games). Nothing is decided either way, so every row is grey.

Format: 12 groups of 4 · top 2 from each group + 8 best 3rd-placed teams = 32 into the Round of 32.
