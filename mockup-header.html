Design Notes

  • Full-bleed: Header extends behind the status bar and nav title all the way to the top of the screen — dark background makes white status bar text pop.
  • Electric pitch: Neon green field lines (center circle, penalty boxes, corner arcs, outer border) on deep dark green. Lines glow/pulse subtly.
  • Shimmer: Diagonal light sweep across the entire header every 3.5 seconds.
  • Version A (Bouncing Ball): ⚽ floats around the header background on a looping path with a subtle green glow trail. Ball is slightly transparent so it doesn't compete with the text.
  • Version B (Static Ball): ⚽ sits before "MatchMondo" in the nav title with a neon green drop-shadow. Clean and simple — matches the HTML site style.
  • Text glow: "MatchMondo" title has a subtle green text-shadow to feel electric/neon.
  • Match count: "23 of 104 matches played" added as a third line in lighter opacity.
