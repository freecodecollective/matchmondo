MatchMondo

One idea, six ways: the soccer ball slowly rotates and transforms into a globe, then back again. Compare the treatments, then pick the one that should become the loading + splash mark.

v1Cross-Dissolve Spin

Both faces share one slow spin; the ball dissolves into the globe under a fixed highlight. Calm, premium, safest for a logo.

v23D Coin Flip

A true rotateY flip — ball on the front, globe on the back. Crisp and physical; the most "transform" of the set.

v3Barrel Roll

The surface scrolls past the horizon like a rolling planet — ball wraps into globe wraps into ball. Continuous, hypnotic.

v4Seam → Graticule

The ball's pentagon seams fade into lat/long lines as white turns ocean-blue. The "aha" version — same geometry, two meanings.

v5Iris Reveal

The globe blooms outward from the center over the spinning ball, then closes back. Playful, great for a tap/launch moment.
