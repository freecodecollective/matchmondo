Motion Lab · mark exploration

MatchMondo

One idea, twenty-two ways: the soccer ball slowly rotates and transforms into a globe, then back again. v1–v6 explore different motion treatments; v7–v10 explore art direction; v11 uses real photos with SVG-bridged rotation frames; v12 uses only the real photos, no SVG bridges; v6a–v6j are 10 emoji-morph variations on different spin/dissolve treatments — photoreal, sketch, watercolour, flat poster. Compare and pick the one that should become the loading + splash mark.

v1Cross-Dissolve Spin

Both faces share one slow spin; the ball dissolves into the globe under a fixed highlight. Calm, premium, safest for a logo.

v23D Coin Flip

A true rotateY flip — ball on the front, globe on the back. Crisp and physical; the most "transform" of the set.

v3Barrel Roll

The surface scrolls past the horizon like a rolling planet — ball wraps into globe wraps into ball. Continuous, hypnotic.

v4Seam → Graticule

The ball's pentagon seams fade into lat/long lines as white turns ocean-blue. The "aha" version — same geometry, two meanings.

v5Iris Reveal

The globe blooms outward from the center over the spinning ball, then closes back. Playful, great for a tap/launch moment.

v6Emoji Morph
⚽
🌍

Zero-asset fallback: ⚽ spins down and 🌍 spins up with a blur cross-fade. Drop-in for any platform, no SVG required.

v7Photoreal 3D

A serious render: specular highlight, cream stitching, atmospheric glow, and continent silhouettes drawn from a real Earth projection. The "buy this for the App Store icon" candidate.

v8Pen & Ink Sketch

Hand-drawn on cream paper: wobbled SVG strokes via a turbulence filter, light pencil hatching on the shadow side, no fills. Reads as field-notes / sketchbook.

v9Watercolor

Pigment bleeds across the panels via displacement + Gaussian blur. Muted, painterly, second-pass wash overlays. Editorial / matchday-programme energy.

v10Modern Flat Poster

Three-colour poster: cream paper, ink seams, MatchMondo grass continents, heavy outline. Strong at any size; reproduces in a single ink pass.

v11Real Photo + Rotating Earth

The real thing: actual ball photo crossfades through five rotation frames — Africa, Asia, Pacific, Americas — and back. Africa + Americas frames are real photos in the reference style; Asia + Pacific are SVG bridges drawn to match. Looks like the globe is genuinely spinning.

v12Real Photos Only

No SVG bridges — just the three real photos crossfaded directly. The globe alternates between your Africa-centered and Americas-centered photos (~90° apart). More honest to the source material; less continuous as a rotation than V11.

v6aEmoji — Pure Crossfade
⚽
🌍

Calmest of the set: opacity only, no rotation, no scale, no blur. ⚽ fades through to 🌍 and back. The cleanest read; works at any size.

v6bEmoji — Continuous Sync Spin
⚽
🌍

Both emojis spin together at the same rate, same direction, with a mid-cycle opacity swap. Feels like one object rotating on a fixed axis, just changing texture mid-spin.

v6cEmoji — Counter-Spin Juggle
⚽
🌍

Ball spins clockwise; globe spins counter-clockwise. They pass each other in opposite directions at the handoff — playful, like a juggling toss.

v6dEmoji — Elastic Bounce
⚽
🌍

Squash & pop with elastic easing — no rotation. Ball squashes to .85, vanishes, globe springs in past 1× and settles. Quirky, app-icony.

v6eEmoji — Zoom Punch
⚽
🌍

Cinematic: emoji enters from huge with heavy blur, snaps to focus, then exits to tiny with blur. Maximum motion energy of the set.

v6fEmoji — 3D Coin Flip
⚽
🌍

Genuine 3D rotateY — the ball is on the front face, globe on the back. Tap the perspective: it looks like flipping a coin between the two.

v6gEmoji — Dribble Bounce
⚽
🌍

Stays centered and bounces vertically — two small hops in sequence, like dribbling a ball. Crossfades to globe mid-cycle; globe bounces too. In-bounds, app-icon-friendly.

v6hEmoji — Header Traverse
⚽
🌍

Rolls across the header from left to right with a small vertical bounce along the path. Ball completes the traverse, then globe takes over and continues. Stays in bounds.

v6iEmoji — Pulse Beat
⚽
🌍

Heartbeat pulse — subtle scale (1× ↔ 1.08×) at a steady rhythm. No translation, no rotation. Calmest motion of the set; centered the whole time.

v6jEmoji — Snap Quick
⚽
🌍

Quick & sharp — half-rotation, no blur, no scale. Sharp dwell at each end with a snappy mid-cycle switch. Tap/UI-button energy.

Studio note — for the brand idea, v4 (Seam → Graticule) still argues hardest that a soccer ball is a globe. For absolute realism, v11 (Real Photo + Rotating Earth) is the standout — it crossfades three real photos with two SVG bridges so the globe genuinely rotates. v7 (Photoreal SVG) remains the all-vector production candidate — real specular, atmospheric ring, recognisable continent silhouettes. v8 (Pen & Ink) and v9 (Watercolour) are editorial / programme-cover energy. v10 (Flat Poster) is the one-ink-pass version. All twenty-two honour prefers-reduced-motion and the global Pause / Speed controls above.
