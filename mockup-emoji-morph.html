Eleven variations on the emoji morph: the soccer ball ⚽ rotates and transforms into the globe 🌍, then back. All share the same Pause and Rotation Speed controls. Transition Speed controls how sharp the morph itself feels — low = gentle blend, high = snappy switch. Turn Bouncing on to add a global up-and-down bounce on top of each variant's own motion — the Bounce Speed slider controls how fast it bobs.

v6Emoji — Original
⚽
🌍

The classic from the lab — spin-down ball cross-fades into spin-up globe with a blur swap mid-cycle. The starting point everything else iterates on.

v6aEmoji — Pure Crossfade
⚽
🌍

Calmest of the set: opacity only, no rotation, no scale, no blur. ⚽ fades through to 🌍 and back. The cleanest read; works at any size.

v6bEmoji — Continuous Sync Spin
⚽
🌍

Both emojis spin together at the same rate, same direction, with a mid-cycle opacity swap. Feels like one object rotating on a fixed axis, just changing texture mid-spin.

v6cEmoji — Counter-Spin Juggle
⚽
🌍

Ball spins clockwise; globe spins counter-clockwise. They pass each other in opposite directions at the handoff — playful, like a juggling toss.

v6dEmoji — Elastic Bounce
⚽
🌍

Squash & pop with elastic easing — no rotation. Ball squashes to .85, vanishes, globe springs in past 1× and settles. Quirky, app-icony.

v6eEmoji — Zoom Punch
⚽
🌍

Cinematic: emoji enters from huge with heavy blur, snaps to focus, then exits to tiny with blur. Maximum motion energy of the set.

v6fEmoji — 3D Coin Flip
⚽
🌍

Genuine 3D rotateY — the ball is on the front face, globe on the back. Tap the perspective: it looks like flipping a coin between the two.

v6gEmoji — Dribble Bounce
⚽
🌍

Stays centered and bounces vertically — two small hops in sequence, like dribbling a ball. Independent of the global bounce; crossfades to globe mid-cycle.

v6hEmoji — Header Traverse
⚽
🌍

Rolls across the header from left to right with a small vertical bounce along the path. Ball completes the traverse, then globe takes over and continues. Stays in bounds.

v6iEmoji — Pulse Beat
⚽
🌍

Heartbeat pulse — subtle scale (1× ↔ 1.08×) at a steady rhythm. No translation, no rotation. Calmest motion of the set; centered the whole time.

v6jEmoji — Snap Quick
⚽
🌍

Quick & sharp — half-rotation, no blur, no scale. Sharp dwell at each end with a snappy mid-cycle switch. Tap/UI-button energy.

Studio note — v6 Original, v6a Pure Crossfade, and v6j Snap Quick are the safest production picks. v6f 3D Coin Flip reads as the most distinctive. v6g Dribble Bounce and v6h Header Traverse are tuned for "ball moving along a header" energy. The global Bouncing toggle composes on top of any variant.
