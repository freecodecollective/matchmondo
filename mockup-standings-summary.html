📊 Standings summary — clickable options

Right now the Through / Out / In-contention cards aren’t tappable. Here are four ways they could become interactive. Every demo below is live — tap the cards. (Sample data, 2 groups.)

The question: when you tap “3 Through” (or Out / In contention), what should happen? Try each option and pick a favorite — or mix ideas. — for Luis
AHighlight in the tables
Tapping a card keeps you in the group tables and dims every team that isn’t in that bucket — so you see exactly where the through / out teams sit. Tap again to clear.
Good for: seeing position in context, no navigation. Trade-off: with many groups you still scroll to find them.
BExpand a list inline
Tapping a card opens a little panel right under the cards listing those teams (flag, name, group). Quick “who exactly?” without touching the tables. Tap again to close.
Good for: a fast roll-call. Trade-off: a second list separate from the tables.
COpen a bottom sheet
Tapping a card slides up a sheet listing the teams, each row tappable through to the team page. Best when the list is long (e.g. 42 in contention).
Good for: long lists + tappable rows to teams. Trade-off: covers the standings while open.
DFilter to only those teams
Tapping a card filters the tables down to just that bucket, with a “Showing: Through · Show all” bar. A focus mode. Tap “Show all” to reset.
Good for: a clean focused view. Trade-off: loses the full-group context while filtered.
