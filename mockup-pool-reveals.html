Pool Reveals — what did everyone pick, and how did they score?

Two new views that open up once picks are locked and matches are underway. Screen A shows everyone's picks for a single match (the "big reveal" moment). Screen B shows your personal scoring breakdown — match-by-match, which points you earned and which you missed. Screen C is the same idea but for the full pool — tap any match to expand everyone's picks + points.

1 · After kickoff: the reveal

Once a match kicks off, picks lock and everyone can see what everyone else predicted. Tap any match from the pool to see this.

Picks revealed, no scores yet. The tension builds as the match unfolds.
