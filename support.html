About

MatchMondo is a free app for following the FIFA World Cup 2026. It shows live scores, full schedule, group standings, player profiles, and tournament rules.

FAQ

How often do scores update?

Scores refresh automatically every 60 seconds. You can also pull down on any screen to refresh manually.

Why don't I see scores for a match?

Scores appear once a match has started and results are confirmed. Upcoming matches show "vs" until kickoff.

Can I hide spoilers?

Yes! Tap the eye icon on the Today or Schedule tabs to toggle scores on or off.

Where does the data come from?

Match data is sourced from official FIFA schedules and updated throughout the tournament.

Does the app work offline?

MatchMondo requires an internet connection to fetch the latest scores and schedule data.
