ⓘ How to play

The Daily Mini takes about a minute.

• Tap a square to select it; tap again to switch between Across and Down.
• Type with the on-screen keyboard (or your computer keyboard).
• Use ‹ › to move between clues, or tap a clue to jump.
• Stuck? The 🎯 menu lets you check or reveal.
• Solve it and you'll get a “What you learned” card for every answer — a new fact about the beautiful game.

A fresh Mini drops every matchday of Football 2026.
