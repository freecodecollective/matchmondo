⚽ Formations — ESPN-style, two tabs

One tab per team, each on the full pitch (so there's room for headshots + numbers), positioned the way ESPN does it. Today's games below — tap the red button on each to open ESPN and compare.

Why this fixes it The old view crammed both teams onto one pitch and ordered players by formation size, which scrambled them (ESPN's formationPlace isn't a simple back-to-front sequence, and some feeds only give coarse D/M/F positions). Here each player is dropped onto ESPN's exact grid slot for their formation + place — so it matches ESPN one-to-one. Tap a team tab to switch sides.
