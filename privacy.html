MatchMondo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Overview

MatchMondo is a free World Cup 2026 schedule and scores app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. MatchMondo does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use.

Data We Do Not Collect

MatchMondo does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints

Network Requests

The app fetches publicly available World Cup match data from GitHub (github.com) and flag images from flagcdn.com. These requests do not include any personal or identifying information. These third-party services may log standard server access information (IP addresses) per their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

MatchMondo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

MatchMondo does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
