MatchMondo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Overview

MatchMondo is a free football schedule and scores app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. MatchMondo does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use.

We use Google Firebase to collect two categories of anonymous, non-personal data:

Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

When the app crashes, we automatically collect diagnostic information to help us fix bugs. This includes:

  • Stack traces and crash logs
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • A random, anonymous installation identifier (not tied to your identity)

Crash data contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you.

Usage Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We collect anonymous usage data to understand how people use the app and improve the experience. This includes:

  • Which screens are viewed and how often
  • Session duration and frequency
  • General device and OS information

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not track individual users, and we do not use advertising identifiers.

Data We Do Not Collect

MatchMondo does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Advertising IDs or cross-app tracking identifiers
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints

Network Requests

The app fetches publicly available match data from GitHub (github.com) and flag images from flagcdn.com. These requests do not include any personal or identifying information. These third-party services may log standard server access information (IP addresses) per their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

MatchMondo uses Google Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics) for crash reporting and anonymous usage analytics. Firebase processes data under Google's Firebase Privacy Policy. We do not use any advertising, tracking, or marketing services.

Children's Privacy

MatchMondo does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The anonymous crash and analytics data described above does not identify any individual user.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
