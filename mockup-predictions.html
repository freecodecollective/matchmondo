My quick take (totally open to yours)

Where it lives: I lean toward a dedicated "Predictions" screen under More (Option A) as the home for it, because picks + scores + community % need room to breathe. Option B's "flip a setting and predict right from the Schedule" is a great shortcut we could add on top — they're not mutually exclusive.

Score predictions: the +/− steppers work everywhere and keep it one-tap fast. Scoring (Option C) is what makes scores matter — exact score +3, right result +1.

Keeping the data: picks save automatically — no login to play. To join a friends' group (Option C) you just enter a two-word code like brave-falcon or tap the invite link and pick a display name — still no account. The only thing that needs a real login is syncing your own picks across two of your devices. (Shared leaderboards do need a tiny backend, since they're visible to everyone — see the "How invites work" section below.)
