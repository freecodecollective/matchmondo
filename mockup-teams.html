Design Notes

  • Sort Controls: Three pill buttons — By Rank (default, current behavior), A → Z (alphabetical), By Group (adds group headers). Active pill is green.
  • Coach: New card below the header showing coach name and nationality flag.
  • No Spoilers Mode: Yellow banner explains what's hidden. Stats row and schedule are CSS-blurred. Roster remains visible (no spoilers there). "Tap to reveal" for manual override.
  • Roster-First: All players shown equally in the squad list — no "key players" vs "rest" split. Sorted by number within position groups (GK → DF → MF → FW). Each row shows jersey number, name, club, and international caps.
  • Clickable Players: Tapping a player expands to show: (1) a 2-3 sentence bio with a personal story or fun fact, (2) career stats (caps, goals, assists, WC appearances), (3) 2026 WC stats (minutes, goals, assists, touches, tackles, key passes/clearances).
  • Bio Examples: Bellingham's youngest scorer record, Roberto Lopes' LinkedIn message to Cape Verde FA. The idea is every player gets something human — the AI can find a story for everyone.
  • WC Stats: Grid layout. Stats shown depend on position — defenders show clearances, midfielders show key passes, forwards show shots. All get minutes, goals, assists, touches, tackles.
