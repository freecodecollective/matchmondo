Puma$100 🏆 Ranked #1 of all 48 kits — The New York Times
A crisp white shirt taken over by a giant multicoloured cobweb — the web of Kwaku Ananse, the trickster spider of Ghanaian folklore and a symbol of wisdom and creativity. Kente-inspired geometric accents run through it in red, gold and green, with the Black Star anchored at the centre and flag-coloured cuffs. Created under the creative direction of Ghanaian artist Prince Gyasi.
