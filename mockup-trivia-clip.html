🎬 Trivia clips — clickable options

When a trivia answer has a video, how should the clip look and behave? Each option below opens the clip. Pick the one you like — for Luis.

In the app: tapping a clip opens it in the YouTube app (or the browser if YouTube isn't installed) — it no longer tries to play inside MatchMondo, which was causing errors. These mocks open YouTube in a new tab so you can feel the behavior. The question/answer below is just sample context.
