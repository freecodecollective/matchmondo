Match Time-Lapse — scrub 0′→90′

Jahan's idea: a slider under the formation pitch. Drag it to watch the lineup change through the match — subs come on/off, goals & cards pop at the exact minute, score & clock update live, and each goal replays the real shot (from → to). Built on real Netherlands 5–1 Sweden data.
