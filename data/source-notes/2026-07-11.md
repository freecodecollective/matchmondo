## Spain 2-1 Belgium QF Recap

### Result
- Mikel Merino (Arsenal) scored the winner in the 88th minute *(Men in Blazers, Davo)*
- Fabian Ruiz gave Spain the lead on the half-hour mark *(Men in Blazers, Davo)*
- Charles De Ketelaere scored a beautifully worked Belgium equalizer -- the first goal Spain conceded all tournament *(Men in Blazers, Davo)*

### Merino Record
- Merino became the first player in tournament history to score the winner in two different knockout-stage matches as a substitute *(Men in Blazers, Davo)*

### Belgium's Winner / Onana Error
- Manchester United keeper Andre Onana came off the bench to replace an injured Thibaut Courtois; fumbled the ball which led to the winning goal *(Men in Blazers, Davo)*
- Rory (Night Cup): "It was a regulation save. He should have caught it. It did bounce quite awkwardly... but for a professional that will be devastating." *(Men in Blazers, Rory)*
