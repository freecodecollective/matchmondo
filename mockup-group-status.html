The 3rd-place race — who fills the last 8 spots? The 12 teams that finish 3rd in their group, ranked head-to-head. Top 8 advance; bottom 4 go home.

Yes — you can absolutely show this, and it’s exactly how the format resolves. The 12 third-placed teams are ranked by points → goal difference → goals scored. It’s a live standing, not a forecast: it stays provisional (and can shuffle) until every group has played the same number of games.

In this example, Korea Republic grabs the 8th and final spot over Belgium — both finished on 3 pts with a 0 goal difference, so it came down to goals scored (2 vs 1). That razor-thin cut is exactly why a live best-3rd table is fun to watch.
