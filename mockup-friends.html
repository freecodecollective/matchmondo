How it works (the NYT model)

  • No groups. You just have friends. Share your code (or enter a friend's) and you're connected — both ways.
  • Friends-of-friends aren't pooled. Your friends can add their own friends; everyone sees their own circle, not one shared pool.
  • One leaderboard, two games. Toggle Overall · Predictions · Trivia to compare on each — Overall is the two added together.
  • Codes are two short football words/players with a dash — e.g. saka-volley, kane-net, vini-chip. Unique per person, easy to read aloud or text.
  • Reachable from its own row under the More tab — separate from making picks.
