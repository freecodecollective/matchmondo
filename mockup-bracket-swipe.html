🏆 Knockout bracket — swipe

Sweep left ↔ right to slide through the rounds. It snaps to each round and the bar up top tracks where you are — like ESPN’s March Madness bracket.

Try it: drag/sweep the bracket sideways, tap a round pill to jump, or use the ◀ ▶ arrows. Winners carry a green ↗ to show who advances. (Projected sample.)
