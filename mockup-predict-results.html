👋 Hi Heather — two questions on Predictions

1) What happens after a game ends? Right now the pick just disappears (matches drop off once they kick off). The proposal: a finished pick becomes a graded result — your prediction vs. the final, marked Exact (nailed the score), Right result, or Missed — shown in a new "Your results" section instead of vanishing.

2) Spoilers. Some people open the app before watching a recorded match. The app already has a global Scores setting (hide / show) that the Schedule and Match pages respect. The three options below differ only in how Predictions handles that. Tap the blurred results to reveal; on Option C, flip the toggle.

Shared in all 3

After a game, your pick shows openly (your own pick isn't a spoiler) and becomes a graded result. Only the final score + grade are treated as spoilers.

My quick take (open to yours)

After a game: turn the pick into a graded "Your results" entry (Exact / Right result / Missed) so people see how they did instead of the pick silently disappearing — all three options do this.

Spoilers — I lean Option A. The app already has one Scores setting that Schedule and Match detail respect; Predictions should obey the same rule so there's a single, predictable control — plus tap-any-result to peek. A second, separate toggle (Option C) is the most flexible but risks two competing spoiler controls that disagree. Option B is the safest but ignores people who've turned spoilers on on purpose.
